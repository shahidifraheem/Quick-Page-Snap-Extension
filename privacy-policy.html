Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/04/2025

Thank you for using Quick Page Snap. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

Quick Page Snap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data.

  • We do not track your activity.
  • We do not store screenshots or image content on any external servers.
  • All screenshots and editing happen locally in your browser.

2. Permissions Explained

This extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To capture the visible content of the current tab.

These permissions are used only to enable core functionality. No data is shared or sent externally.

3. Data Storage

All processing and editing are done in your browser. No data is uploaded or stored remotely.

4. Third-Party Services

Quick Page Snap does not use any third-party services, trackers, or analytics.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will be reflected on the Chrome Web Store or shared via extension updates.
